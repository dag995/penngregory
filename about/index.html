About Penn Gregory

Penn Gregory is a collaboration between two experienced recruitment professionals, offering over 50 years of experience - assisting hundreds of clients and candidates to enjoy a fruitful recruitment experience.

Rest assured, these old dogs have learnt new tricks, so, coupled with loads of recruitment knowledge, much investment in the latest resourcing techniques has been embraced, ensuring a market leading service for client and candidate alike.

Simon Penn

With over 30 years in the recruitment industry, Simon is a seasoned professional and Co-Founder of a highly successful, independent agency he helped grow from inception in 2001 to a multi-office business with over 80 staff and a £14m turnover. His expertise combines strategic vision with a hands-on approach, fostering collaboration, excellence, and long-term client relationships. Passionate about delivering first-class service, Simon is dedicated to building sustainable business relationships through honesty, innovation, and people-focused values.

Simon relishes going back to a full time, hands-on role dedicated to personally ensuring clients and candidates benefit from a cost effective, first class recruitment experience.

Greg Oakes

Greg Oakes is an accomplished recruitment leader with over 25 years of experience driving business growth and leading high-performing teams across Technology, Engineering, Financial Services, and the Third Sector. He specialises in strategic client development, complex talent delivery, and long-term partnership management.
